# NAIR DEMAKE

Wasm4 (RUST) demake of NAIR, a simple fighting game with only one attack.
Source: https://codeberg.org/MiguelGSS/NAIR-Demake

---

A demake of "[NAIR](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1715850/NAIR/)", with simplified controls.
Z: The NAIR button, only works while jumping;
X: Dodge button. It has two versions: 
- On the floor, it can go to left, right or just stay on the same spot. It has less cooldown then the air version.
- While jumping, you can dodge to 8 different directions, and can also just dodge on the spot making you stay a little more in the air.
Up: Jump button and wall jump button.
Down: Crouch, and can make you fall faster.
Left and right: Moves to left or right. Can be used any time you're not taking damage, dodging or crouching.
